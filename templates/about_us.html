About
Yurt Horizon

More than travel. A journey into the soul of Kyrgyzstan.

At Yurt Horizon, we don’t just organize tours — we craft life-changing experiences across Kyrgyzstan’s untouched landscapes. Our passion lies in connecting travelers with the deep-rooted traditions, raw beauty, and nomadic warmth of this ancient land.
Founded by locals, driven by heart, and committed to excellence, we believe in slow travel, real encounters, and unforgettable memories. From shimmering alpine lakes and yurt stays to eagle hunting shows and mountain dinners — everything we offer is authentic, curated, and deeply personal.
Whether you seek solitude in highland silence or adventure on winding passes, our mission is simple: To make you feel not like a tourist, but like a guest of Kyrgyzstan.

Our Story

Born from the mountains. Built with love. Guided by purpose.

Yurt Horizon began as a dream shared by two lifelong friends — Azat and Nurbek — who grew up with the rhythm of the mountains in their hearts. One had the vision, the other had the drive, and both were united by a deep love for their land.
What started as a simple idea — to show travelers the true Kyrgyzstan — became a mission to create something rare:

A tour company that feels like family. A journey that feels like home. An experience that stays with you long after the road ends.
Every route we design, every guest we welcome, every story we share — is a reflection of who we are: honest, warm, rooted in tradition, and always striving for excellence.
This is not just a business. This is our calling. And we’re honored to walk this path with you.
